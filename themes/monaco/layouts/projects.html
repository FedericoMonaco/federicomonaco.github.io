RISVO
2017–2024

Open Data for Infection Risk

University of Parma · SimLab · ANIPIO · OnData APS

Public engagement and promotion project on open data for infection risk in healthcare settings. Developed in collaboration with ANIPIO (National Association of Hospital Infection Prevention Nurses) and OnData APS, the project promotes transparent, open data practices for the public administration in healthcare surveillance. A model now being developed into a broader mental health observatory.

SimHub
2017–2024

Open Medical Simulation Platform

SimLab · Dept. Medicine & Surgery · University of Parma

Open-source platform with Creative Commons-licensed kits containing interactive scenarios for medical and emergency simulation. Designed to democratise access to simulation learning for community healthcare workers, students and citizens. Developed in collaboration with the Municipality of Parma's Welfare Assessment office as part of a social inclusion initiative.

Open Source Creative Commons Medical Simulation Emergency Training Social Inclusion

360°
2017–2024

Virtual Simulation for Remote Medical Training

UniPR · SimLab

Research and development in VR and digital education for remote medical-surgical simulation. Accessible 360° immersive environments that allow clinical training without requiring specialist hardware or physical presence — a key innovation for distributed healthcare training.
