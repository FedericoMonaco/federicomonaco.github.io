Who I Am

Connecting dots others don't see

I am a sociologist by training — with a PhD from the University of Parma on the intersections of matter, knowledge and society — and a practitioner by vocation. My career has woven together academic research, digital innovation, healthcare simulation, e-learning strategy and civic engagement into a coherent practice: helping organisations and communities navigate complexity by designing shared futures.

Based in Parma, Italy, I work at the intersection of public institutions and emerging technologies. I design learning environments that are open, accessible and genuinely human.

I collaborate with universities, hospitals, schools, regional innovation agencies and international research networks — always with the conviction that lasting digital transformation is cultural before it is technical.
